❓ Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is an instant personal loan?

An instant personal loan is a digital loan that can be approved and disbursed quickly through mobile apps or websites.

2. How much loan can I get?

Loan amount depends on income, credit profile and lender policies.

3. Is PAN card mandatory?

Yes, most lenders require PAN verification.

4. Is Aadhaar required?

Yes, Aadhaar is generally required for KYC verification.

5. How long does approval take?

Many apps provide approval within minutes.

6. Can students apply?

Some apps provide loans for students, subject to eligibility.

7. Do I need a salary account?

Not always. Many lenders accept regular bank accounts.

8. What is the minimum credit score?

Requirements vary by lender. Some apps also lend to new borrowers.

9. Can I repay early?

Many lenders allow foreclosure or early repayment.

10. Is D-LOAN OFFERS a lender?

No. We are a loan comparison and aggregation platform.
